Hello, we are super excited that you were curious about our game. We had started it in the summer of 2017 during our computer science program GIrls Who Code. It is a small stigma game that represents how different girls and women around the world face their menstruation cycle. Although most of the levels are females based in The U.S., I still feel that some cases are still relatable in the most industrial countries and/or developing countries. This game also helps people imagine how differing environments can change one’s experience. I hope this gives a little in the thought behind our game.
